Welcome to KernelFlow Linux

What is KernelFlow?

KernelFlow Linux (abbreviated as KFL for short) is an upcoming lightweight Linux distribution targeted towards lower end systems and most daily tasks. KernelFlow was created by nqoss and koldfreg as a result of poor performance in other Linux distros.

What makes us different?

Our OS strives to perform at its peak power. It's based on Arch Linux and includes many different DEs, including:

KDE Plasma, GNOME, KDE Plasma, XFCE, LXQt, LXDE
